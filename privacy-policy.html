Privacy Policy for Crosswatch

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

TL;DR: Crosswatch does not collect, store, or share any personal data. All your workout data stays on your device.

1. Introduction

Crosswatch ("we", "our", or "the app") is a workout timer application designed to help users with HIIT, CrossFit, and circuit training workouts. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

2. Data Collection

Crosswatch does not collect any personal information. The app operates entirely offline and does not:

  • Collect personal identification information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Track your location
  • Access your contacts, photos, or other personal files
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Contain advertising networks
  • Transmit any data over the internet

3. Local Storage

All workout data you create in Crosswatch is stored locally on your device using secure storage mechanisms provided by your operating system. This includes:

  • Custom workout routines you create
  • Workout settings and preferences
  • App configuration settings

This data never leaves your device and is only accessible to the Crosswatch app on your device.

4. Permissions

Crosswatch may request the following permissions:

  • Notifications: To alert you when workout intervals complete (optional, can be disabled)
  • Wake Lock: To keep your screen on during workouts

These permissions are used solely for app functionality and do not involve data collection.

5. Third-Party Services

Crosswatch does not integrate with any third-party services, SDKs, or APIs that could collect data.

6. Children's Privacy

Crosswatch does not collect any data from anyone, including children under 13. The app is safe for all ages.

7. Data Security

Since no data is collected or transmitted, there is no risk of data breaches or unauthorized access to your information through our app. Your workout data remains secure on your device, protected by your device's security measures.

8. Changes to This Policy

If we make changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. Any changes will be posted here and, if significant, will be communicated through the app or app store listing.

9. Your Rights

Since we do not collect any data, there is no data to access, modify, or delete on our servers. You have complete control over your workout data stored on your device and can delete it at any time by:

  • Deleting individual workouts within the app
  • Clearing app data through your device settings
  • Uninstalling the app
